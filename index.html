Fair Play First

Rules & Guidelines

We run zero-tolerance, fair-play tournaments. Know the rules before you register.

All players must compete on legitimate, unmodified game clients. Any form of cheating results in immediate disqualification and a permanent ban from all future Nexus BG events.

  • No aimbots, wallhacks, or lag-switch tools
  • No macros or scripting tools (for Valorant PC)
  • Emulators strictly prohibited in mobile tournaments
  • Screen-sharing may be required if suspected

All registered players must provide accurate in-game IDs. Mismatch between form data and in-game name may result in disqualification.

  • Free Fire: In-game UID (numeric)
  • BGMI: Player ID (numeric) visible in-game
  • Valorant: Full Riot ID including tag (#XXXX)
  • Admin reserves the right to request profile screenshots

Room ID and password are shared exclusively in our Discord server 30 minutes before match start. Late entries forfeit their slot.

  • All players must join the custom room within 5 minutes of sharing
  • No replacements after lobby is locked
  • Match starts on time — late teams are auto-eliminated
  • Valorant lobby invites sent 20 mins before scheduled time

The following will result in immediate disqualification without appeal:

  • Use of any cheat software or modified client
  • Teaming with other squads (in BR formats)
  • Toxic, abusive behavior toward other participants or admins
  • Sharing Room ID/password with non-registered players
  • No-show without prior notice 24 hrs in advance

Battle Royale (FF / BGMI):

  • 1st: 15 pts | 2nd: 12 pts | 3rd: 10 pts
  • 4th–6th: 8 pts | 7th–10th: 4 pts
  • +1 point per elimination (kill)
  • Tie-break: higher kills → earlier finish rank

Results are announced within 2 hours of the final match via:

  • Official Discord #results channel
  • Instagram story (top 3 only)
  • Winners contacted via Discord DM within 24 hrs
  • Prize distribution within 3–5 business days

All prizes are distributed via UPI / bank transfer to the team captain's verified account.

  • Team captain must provide valid UPI ID or account details
  • Prize will not be distributed if ID verification fails
  • TDS may apply as per Indian IT laws for prizes above ₹10,000
  • Disputes must be raised within 12 hours of result announcement

The Nexus BG admin team has final authority on all in-tournament disputes. Admin decisions are binding and final once announced.

  • Disputes must be submitted in the #support channel
  • Video/screenshot evidence is required for any claim
  • Harassment of admins results in immediate ban
  • Nexus BG reserves the right to modify formats if required
